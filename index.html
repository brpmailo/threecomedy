Welcome to 3Comedy-Team

Our mission is simple — to make people smile. From relatable Nepali moments to original funny sketches, we bring you laughter straight from our hearts. Stay tuned and subscribe for our latest content! 🎬

About Us

3Comedy-Team is a creative group of passionate individuals who believe that laughter is the best therapy. Inspired by our hit Bhatabhungga Serial, we bring you hilarious stories featuring our main characters: Mailo, Gyane Don, Bibek, and Nabin, each adding their own unique humor and charm.

We started our journey with a simple dream — to make people forget their worries, even if it’s just for a few minutes. Through our short comedy videos, skits, and the Bhatabhungga Serial, we aim to connect with audiences across Nepal and beyond.

Our team works tirelessly behind the scenes to write, shoot, edit, and deliver quality content that reflects real-life situations in a funny and heartwarming way. Whether it's daily life humor, social satire, or comedic adventures, our goal is to spread positivity, laughter, and joy everywhere we go. 🎭✨
